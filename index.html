Forest Coverage in World

This visualisation has visualised the forest coverage in the world.

Forest Coverage of Countries 2020

This choropleth map has showed the forest coverage of countries in 2020. From this map, we can observe that the data of some places does not exist. Furthermore, the forest coverage is higher at the equatorial area.

Forest Coverage in 10 Most Populous Countries



From this graph we can observe that Indonesia's forest coverage was decreased the most between 1990 to 2020. However, the forest coverage of Bangladesh, Russia and United States of America does not change much.

Forest Area of 10 Least Populous Countries




For this bar graph, we can select the year to observe and can find that Palau has the largest forest area and Tuvalu has the smallest forest area among the 10 least populous countries between 1991 to 2020.

Forest Area of 10 Largest Countries




For this bar graph, we can select the year to observe and can find that Russia has the largest forest area and Algeria has the smallest forest area among the 10 largest countries between 1990 to 2020.

Forest Area Proportion of Continent




For this pie chart, we can select the year to observe and can find that Europe has the largest forest area and Oceania has the smallest forest area among the continents between 1990 to 2020.

Continent Forest Area




For this bar graph, we can select the year to observe and can find that South America's forest area was decreased the most and Asia's forest area was increased the most between 1990 to 2020. Furthermore, Europe has the largest forest area from 1990 to 2020.

Forest Area in World




Overall, we can observe that the forest area in the world was decreased between 1990 to 2020. However, the forest area in the world was increased between 1999 to 2000 and between 2009 to 2011.
