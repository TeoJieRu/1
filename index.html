Forest Coverage in World

This visualisation has visualised the forest coverage in the world.

Forest Coverage of Countries in World

This choropleth map has showed the forest coverage of countries in 2020. From this map, we can observe that the data of some places does not exist. Furthermore, the forest coverage is higher at the equatorial area.

Forest Coverage in the 10 most populous Countries



From this graph we can observe that Indonesia's forest coverage was decreased the most between 1990 to 2020. However, the forest coverage of Bangladesh, Russia and United States of America does not change much.

Continental Forest Area




From this bar graph we can observe that South America's forest area was decreased the most and Asia's forest area was increased the most between 1990 to 2020.

Forest Area in World




Overall, we can observe that the forest area in the world was decreased between 1990 to 2020. However, the forest area in the world was increased between 1999 to 2000 and between 2009 to 2011.
